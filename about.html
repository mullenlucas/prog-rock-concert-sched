PROGRESSIVE ROCK REVIVAL CONCERT 2022

This project is intended to fulfill a capstone exercise from the Microverse program. It intends to make a replica of the CC Global Summit 2015 conference website, using its structure and styling as a template whilst changing and adapting to new content. Said content is fictional and only invented to set an example usage of the template Some of the images used may have rights reserved, the icons were taken from Flat Icon, and the Logo made with freeLogoDesign.
